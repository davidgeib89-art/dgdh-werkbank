and promotion with less manual lane rescue than `DAV-165` / `DAV-166` still needed.

It is a closeout-path mission.

## Bounded Goal

Prove that the same bounded triad shape can survive the worker closeout seam after quota interruption
with less manual intervention from Eidan/Copilot than the first live proof required.

The point is not to make quota disappear.
The point is to make the closing path after quota interruption:

1. more explicit
2. more boring
3. less dependent on manual agent-key/run rescue
4. still reviewer-gated

## Strong Success

`strong success` means all of these are true:

- one bounded triad mission hits the real closeout seam after worker progress
- the worker-to-reviewer-to-promotion closing path carries further than `DAV-166` without equivalent manual rescue
- reviewer verdict remains explicit and still changes the actual path
- the result is durable in runtime truth, repo truth, and operator-facing truth
- the next triad closeout would need less manual execution-lane stitching than before

## Truthful Partial

`truthful partial` means:

- the narrow closeout seam is isolated honestly
- at least one real hardening cut lands on the closeout path
- the remaining blocker is narrow, reviewable, and clearly still on the same seam
- the mission does not widen into a new orchestration platform or quota-fantasy rewrite

## Hard Stop

`hard stop` means:

- the next step crosses a true Type-1 boundary
- the same closeout seam cannot continue without a broader platform/program jump
- or the mission starts solving general autonomy instead of the specific post-tool closeout path

## Guard Metrics

- no AppData / workspaceStorage / chat-session-resources / editor-internal forensics

- no bypassing reviewer truth
- no provider or platform expansion
- no broad runbook/doc churn unless the closeout path actually changes
